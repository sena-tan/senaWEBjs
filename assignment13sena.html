Arrays as JSON

An array is also valid JSON, for example:

        [
            {
                "name": "Jim Alcon",
                "role": "Chief Executive Officer",
                "description": "Jim Alcon is the CEO of our company. He is responsible for the policy and strategy of our company."
            },
            {
                "name": "Bill Peterson",
                "role": "Chief Financial Officer",
                "description": "Bill Peterson is the CFO of our company. He is responsible for all the financial decisions of our company."
            },
            {
                "name": "John Cook",
                "role": "Chef",
                "description": "Our Chef, John Cook has specialized in the production of chocolates and he has created the receipts for our chocolates."
            },
            {
                "name": "Vasilis Wilson",
                "role": "Sous-chef",
                "description": "Vasilis Wilson is well informed of all the current trends in pastry and contributes his innovative ideas in our products."
            }
            
        ]

We want to access this array, so we assign it to the variable "people", in the "script" tag.

Try typing in the console the following commands:

  • people[0]["name"][0]
  • people[0].role
